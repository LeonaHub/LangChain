The text discusses a systematic review and meta-analysis on the association between workplace bullying (WPB) and suicidal ideation and behavior. The study found that individuals exposed to WPB were more likely to report thoughts and behaviors related to suicide. The review included various studies from different countries and identified moderators and mediators that impact this association. The findings suggest that WPB has a significant impact on mental health outcomes, emphasizing the need for further research and interventions to address this issue.
