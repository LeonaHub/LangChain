Policymakers are focusing on integrating care for individuals with both behavioral and physical health conditions, particularly serious mental illnesses, through various national initiatives such as PBHCI, CCBCH, MHH, and PCMH. These initiatives involve multidisciplinary teams, population health management strategies, and financial strategies to target different populations. Challenges in adopting integrated care include a lack of clarity on core components and mixed evidence on outcomes. The initiatives aim to expand services, decision support, self-management support, care management, referrals, coordination, linkages with community services, quality improvement, and sustainability
