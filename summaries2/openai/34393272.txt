The COVID-19 pandemic has significantly impacted the field of chemistry, highlighting the essential role of chemists in developing solutions to combat the virus. While some view this period as defining, others see it as challenging. Chemists like Marina Berzins McCoy stress the importance of human vulnerability in chemistry. Challenges remain in improving the public image of chemistry and increasing interest in the field due to chemophobia, which is an irrational fear of chemical substances. The text emphasizes the need for factually presented success stories to combat chemophobia and promote the benefits of the chemical-pharmaceutical industry. Effective communication is crucial for addressing negative stereotypes and securing support for the field.
