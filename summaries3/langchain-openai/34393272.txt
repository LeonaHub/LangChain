The text discusses the challenges faced by chemistry due to chemophobia and the COVID-19 pandemic, emphasizing the importance of effective communication to improve the public image of chemistry. It introduces a new communication strategy called "Chemists as children of the Sun," highlighting the significant contributions of chemists to healthcare through the discovery of drugs and vaccines. The strategy aims to combat chemophobia by showcasing the positive impact of chemistry on human health and society, using historical examples of chemists who have made groundbreaking discoveries in the field of medicine. The strategy is seen as a universal and powerful tool to enhance the perception of chemistry and promote its importance in addressing global challenges.
