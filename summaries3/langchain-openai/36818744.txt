The text discusses the importance of the Levelized Cost of Electricity (LCOE) in determining the market competitiveness of perovskite photovoltaic (PV) technology. It highlights the challenges in accurately evaluating the LCOE for perovskite PVs, including uncertainties related to performance, stability, and manufacturing costs. The text emphasizes the need for refined models and data to improve LCOE predictions, particularly in areas such as outdoor performance, stability, and manufacturing. It also explores strategies to reduce LCOE, such as improving stability, exploring alternative materials, and increasing production capacity. Overall, the LCOE calculations will play a crucial role in the commercialization and advancement of perovskite PV technology in the coming years.
