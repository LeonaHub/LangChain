The text discusses the association between workplace bullying (WPB) and suicidal ideation and behavior, highlighting the significant impact of WPB on individuals' mental health and well-being. The study conducted a systematic review and meta-analysis to quantify the relationship between WPB and suicidal thoughts and behaviors. The findings suggest that individuals exposed to WPB are at a higher risk of experiencing suicidal ideation and behavior. Various moderators and mediators were identified in this relationship, including factors like social resources, personal resilience, and perceived burdensomeness. The study also emphasized the importance of future research using diverse methodologies to further understand and address the detrimental effects of WPB on mental health.
